The Rule of 3

Seminar 3 · Tutorial
A diagnostic principle for the modern age

I

n the contemporary theatre of courtship, where digital conversation has become both prelude and — far too often — substitute for genuine intent, a single empirical rule emerges with sobering clarity: The Rule of 3.

If, after three consecutive calendar days of sustained textual exchange, no definite appointment has been established — no specified time, no chosen venue, no mutual commitment to meet in the physical world — then the rational course of action is unequivocal: disengage.

“She is not occupied. She is not indecisive. She is simply not sufficiently interested — and the keyboard has become her preferred form of amusement.”

Core Tenet

Time is the scarcest resource of a man of value.

Extended textual correspondence without escalation to an in-person encounter constitutes an asymmetrical exchange: your attention and mental bandwidth offered freely, her entertainment secured at negligible cost. This pattern — frequent replies yet perpetual deferral of plans — is not ambiguity; it is disinterest masked as engagement. Boredom, validation-seeking, or mere habit may sustain the dialogue, but none of these motives merit prolonged investment from a grown adult.

Application

After the third day, absent concrete progress toward a date, further messages serve only to prolong an illusion. Cease initiation. Should she re-engage with genuine intent (a specific counter-proposal rather than vague flirtation), the interaction may be reconsidered. Absent such initiative, the matter is resolved by default.

The principle demands neither anger nor elaborate farewell. A simple withdrawal of effort suffices. Your calendar, your purpose, and your dignity remain intact.

Corollary Observations

• Consistent but shallow replies without logistical momentum are the hallmark of recreational texting.
• Phrases such as “I’m bad at texting” or “I’ve been so busy” function as polite deflections far more often than literal truths.
• Reciprocity in effort is non-negotiable; one-sided propulsion of conversation reveals the true distribution of interest.

Product of a female-dominated household. Reluctant field researcher.
We affirm co-existence, mutual respect, and — above all — the refusal to squander finite years on low-probability digital dalliances.
